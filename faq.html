Frequently Asked Questions

How can I invest with Mexgain?

To make a investment you must first become a member of Mexgain. Once you are signed up, you can make your first deposit. All deposits must be made through the Members Area. You can login using the member username and password you receive when signup.

How do I create my Mexgain Account?

It's quite easy and convenient. Navigate through the "Support" button and click on Register (or click on "Signup"), then fill in the registration form and click on "Register".

Which e-currencies do you accept?

We accept BitCoin, Ethereum, Tron, Ripple and many other digital cuurencies on request.

How can I withdraw funds?

Login to your account using your username and password and check the Withdraw section.

How can I change my e-mail address or password?

Log into your mexgain account and click on "Account Information". You can proceed to change your e-mail address and password, then click "Enter".

What if I can't log into my account because I forgot my password?

Click forgot password link, type your username or e-mail and you'll receive your account information.

Does a daily profit pay directly to my bitcoin account?

No, profits are gathered on your mexgain account, so it can be easy to re-invest or withdraw a specific amount and you can also withdraw at anytime.

How do you calculate the interest on my account?

Depending on each plan. Interest on your mexgain account is acquired and credited by percentage to your available balance.

Can I do a direct deposit from my account balance?

Yes! To make a deposit from your assetgain account balance. Simply login into your members account and click on Make Deposit and select the Deposit from Account Balance Radio button.

Can I make an additional deposit to assetgain account once it has been opened?

Yes, you can but all transactions are handled separately.

After I make a withdrawal request, when will the funds be available on my e-currency or BitCoin account?

Funds are usually available within 35 mins.

Can I lose money?

There is a risk involved with investing in all high yield investment programs. However, there are a few simple ways that can help you to reduce the risk of losing more than you can afford to. First, align your investments with your financial goals, in other words, keep the money you may need for the short-term out of more aggressive investments, reserving those investment funds for the money you intend to raise over the long-term.
